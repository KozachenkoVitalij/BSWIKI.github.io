Black Souls I, the first game in the main Black Souls series and the second game in Black Souls Universe[2], is a dark fantasy and souls-like RPG Maker game developed by Sushi Yuusha Toro and released for PC on April 27, 2017.

It inspired by the Great European children's writers: Charles Perrault, the Brothers Grimm, Hans Christian Anderson, Lewis Carroll and some others, also very carefully by the mythology of Lovecraft. It borrowed a lot of things from the Dark Souls series, which is what the title refers to.


There is also DLC accompanying this game; The Black Goat of the Woods a Thousand Young, which focuses on the Black Trial and adds a new quest and ending with it.

Sinopsis

You are an undead who awakened somewhere in the imprisonment of the Lost Empire. You are freed by Jeanne D'Arc, but then both of you are killed by the dragon. Now you have one goal - to get into the belly of the Lost Empire to defeat Cinderella, who spreads the fog that drives everyone crazy, but to get there you need to defeat or enter into a covenant with the Demonic Princesses.

Gameplay

Souls-like mechanics

Background

  • The Knight - High Attack and Defense. Knows techniques for lowering enemy Attack and Defense.
  • The Thief - High Speed. Knows a technique for slaying foes in a single blow. Begins with 1000 bonus Souls.
  • The Sorcerer - High Magic and Resistance. Knows Soul Arrow from the start.

Bonfire

Bonfires are used to restore health and mana, as well as for quick teleportation between locations. Unlike Dark Souls, bonfires do not save the player.

RPG maker

Black Souls I was developed using RPG Maker VX Ace

Weapoms and Armor

The game is equipped with a huge number of different weapons, but the armor cannot be changed. Instead, the player can use 4 rings at the same time. Each weapon not only has individual characteristics, but also adds its own skills. The rings have some kind of effect on the player, like increasing their ATK by 10%.

Skills

There are two types of skills - Mastery and Sorcery. Skills can cause damage to the enemy or inflict some effect on him or the entire team, restore HP or mana, and much more.

Sin

Doing reprehensible things, such as killing innocents or kidnapping women blackens soul with "Sin". If Sin is high enough, this will effect your standing with certain characters, and have the Black Trial chase after you. Purity of Sin will unlock two endings.

Compinions

In total player can have 10 companions in the game. Each of them can help in battle, and they also have different endings. They can be found all over the map and each has its own way of salvation and rapprochement.
